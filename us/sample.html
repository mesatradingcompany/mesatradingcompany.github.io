About Ah Yat

Master Chef 楊貫一 Yeung Koon Yat

Yeung Koon Yat's signature dish Ah Yat's Abalone (or Bao yu) is famous all around the world. The abalone offered can cost an average of US$7200 per kilogram.

Inpsired by the desire to offer an affordable way for familes to enjoy Abalone, Master Chef Ah Yat teamed up with Japanese abalone farmers to create Ah Yat Braised Canned Abalone. Using the master chef's secret recipe, each can uses premium quality ingredients and can be served straight from the can.
